Brisbane swing dancers love dancing to live music and making new friends. We invite you to visit us at the end of May to celebrate World Lindy Hop day, enjoy our fair city and get to know our friendly dancers.

Schedule

Friday Night

Date: Friday 26th May
Time: 8pm - 11.30pm
Venue: The Danish Club, 36 Austin St, Newstead, 4006
Music: Andrew Garton's Pantheon of Swing
Theme: World Lindy Hop day
Put on your dancing shoes and get ready to make some new friends at our inaugural Swing Thing opening night.

Friday Late Night

Date: Friday 26th May
Time: 12am - 4am
Venue: Mad Dance House, 43 Adelaide St, Brisbane City, 4000
Music: Brisbane's Djs
Theme: keep on dancing
Get casual and chill it out with our late night party. We'll add more blues music as the night wears on. BYO drinks.

Saturday Night

Date: Saturday 27th May
Time: 8pm - 11.30pm
Venue: Elements Collective, 17 McLachlan St, Fortitude Valley, 4006
Music: Highline Big Band
Theme: Fancy Pants
Put on your fancier duds and enjoy the stylings of the Highline Big Band. Enjoy a performance from some local dancers and test your skills in our mix'n'match competition.

Venue: Mad Dance House, 43 Adelaide St, Brisbane City, 4000

Sunday Night

Date: Sunday 28th May
Time: 6pm - 9pm
Venue: The Old Musium, 480 Gregory Tce, Bowen Hills, 4006
Music: Queensland Youth Orchestra Big Band
Theme: Supporting the kids
The QYO big band is an annual event here in Brisbane. The musicians are all school or university aged and you can expect their parents in the crowd.

Sunday Late Night

Date: Sunday 28th May
Time: 9pm - 1am
Venue: Mad Dance House, 43 Adelaide St, Brisbane City, 4000
Music: Brisbane's Djs

We'll wind up the weekend with a casual hang by the Brisbane river.

Places to Stay

Most of our venues are around Brisbane City and Fortitude Valley. Check some nearby hotels or find a local host on our facebook page

Transport

Most of our venues are fairly central so if you're visiting Brisbane you can probably rely on walking, taxi's or ubers, or our public transport network. Plan your travel via the Translink Journey Panner and don't forget to purchase a gocard from a newsagent or ticket machine before boarding a bus. If you get into a pickle, there will always be a local dancer happy to give you a lift.


If you're a local or have hired a car be aware that parking may be limited over the weekend. Book your parking ahead of time with Secure-a-Spot
